Granola with Soy Yogurt and Mixed Berries

$1.75
< 5 minutes
Nutritional Breakdown
  • Fat: 26g
  • Protein: 21.8g
  • Carbs: 99.8g
  • Fiber: 24.8g
  • Sugar: 39.5g
  • Calories: 732
With a full serving of protein and fiber, this breakfast should keep you full until lunch. But be careful: depending on which granola you chose it could be a sugar overdose. The berries and chia seeds add vitamins and healthy fats to make it a well balanced meal.
Ingredients
  1. 1 cup of granola
  2. 3/4 cup of soy yogurt
  3. 3/4 cup of frozen mixed berries
  4. 2 tbsp of chia seeds
Notes
  • Granola can be high in sugar, so choose your brand wisely. For more information, check out: bit.ly/ffeerel
  • Depending on what's available in your neighborhood, Soy yogurt can be replaced with soy milk, almond milk, coconut milk, or coconut yogurt
  • Mix in fresh peaches or other seasonal fruit when available
